Subtitles:

Have you ever used a question mark

and an exclamation mark together?

Well, believe it or not there’s actually

a real punctuation mark

designed specifically for that purpose

called an Interrobang!

It’s basically just an exclamation mark

and a question mark combined into one glyph.

It was invented in 1962 and

was meant to be used for

well not surprisingly

exclamatory questions such as

“What are you doing?” or

“Where is my car?”
