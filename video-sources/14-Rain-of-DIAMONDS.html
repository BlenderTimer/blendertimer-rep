    Did you know that it rains diamonds?

    Well, not on Earth,

    but on the four gas planets.

    This is because there is a lot of methane

    in their atmospheres.

    Lightning storms turn methane

    into carbon which hardens as it falls,

    turning into graphite

    and eventually diamonds!

    It's estimated that around 1000 tons

    worth of diamonds rain down

    in just one year!
